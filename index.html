About RDG & Associates

Established in 1981, we have been providing trusted financial services across Maharashtra with integrity, expertise, and personalized attention.

With over four decades of experience, RDG & Associates has established itself as a leading chartered accountancy firm in Kalyan, Maharashtra. Our commitment to excellence and client satisfaction has helped us build lasting relationships with businesses across 15 different industries.

We specialize in providing comprehensive financial solutions including taxation, auditing, business consulting, and startup advisory services. Our expert team ensures compliance, accuracy, and strategic financial planning for sustainable business growth.
